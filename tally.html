How It Works

The different phases of assessment are:

Impact assessment

Assessing the impact of VAT on existing business operations (Transactions, Contracts, ERP and other business process).

Validation & Implementation

Validation of Impact Assessment Report Testing ERP & Transaction mapping in ERP Training to relevant staff

Go Live & Post Implementation

Registration with the VAT authorities Hand holding in transitional provisions & challenges Review of the VAT returns On Call advisory service

Post implementation and ongoing assistance

VAT health check VAT advisory VAT compliance and periodic returns VAT audit assistance and clarifications from authorities TAX advisory and structuring
