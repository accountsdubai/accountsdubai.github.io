What is it

Experience Tally Prime

Tally Prime is a trusted accounting software that is used by over 1.3 million businesses worldwide. Our connection with business communities and tax professionals helps us to continuously develop product capabilities which are more apt to your ways of working.

Simplicity

Install the software, activate license and start running your business on Tally within minutes. There are no prerequisites, procedures or set rules to follow.

Flexibility

Tally Prime adapts to the ways of your business. Flexibility is provided in terms of simplistic configurations which help you to run your business in unexpected situations.

Speed

Get real-time reports with just a click, such as balance sheet, profit & loss statement, stock summary and cash flow projection to name a few. These reports reflect any changes that are made in the data within fractions of seconds.

Reliable

Tally Prime is a resilient software and is immune to virus attacks of any nature. Your data is always safe, secure and up-to-date. It cannot be lost due to any sudden disruptions in the work environment or unreliable infrastructure.

What does it do?

Tally Prime is an Accounting Software that helps you keep track and manage all of your accounts,sales,debts and makes it more easier to handle your business.

Tally's powerful error detection and correction capabilities makes sure that GSTR-1 and GSTR-3B filing done seamlessly.
